# Middle-Market Reporting Emphasis

Overlay narrative describing the reporting-emphasis posture for the middle-market
segment. This is a skill-pack content file specifying what owner-side and regional
reporting leads with for middle-market properties.

## Primary emphases

1. Collections posture. Rolling-month collections rate, delinquency 30+, bad debt,
   and concession rate. A breach in any one is the first question the owner asks.
2. Controllable opex discipline. Payroll, R&M, utilities net of RUBS, turn cost per
   unit, and controllable opex per unit vs. budget and benchmark.
3. Blended trade-out. New-lease trade-out, renewal trade-out, and the blended
   figure. Material divergence between new-lease and renewal trade-out is the
   diagnostic for renewal strategy health.

Target bands for each of these metrics are in
`reference/derived/role_kpi_targets.csv` under the `row_mm_*` prefix rows cited in
overlay.yaml.

## Secondary emphases

4. Occupancy trio. Physical, leased, and economic occupancy with notice exposure.
5. Leasing funnel. Lead response, tour conversion, application conversion,
   approval rate, move-in conversion.
6. Work-order health. Open work orders, aging, repeat rate.

## Tertiary surfaces

7. Staffing vacancy (TPM overlay or self-managed equivalent).
8. Capex spend vs. plan (rolls up from renovation or recap overlays when
   applicable).
9. Renovation yield on cost (only during active value-add).

## Ownership cadence

The minimum cadence is a monthly operating review with a quarterly deep dive. The
monthly review carries the primary emphases; the quarterly adds trending,
cross-property comparison, and forward-look.

## Principle

The monthly narrative explains variance on the primary emphases in plain language,
cites the target band row it measured against, and names the owner of any
remediation item.
