# Self-Managed Mode Overlay

Baseline management-mode overlay. Owner operates directly. The overlay implies:

- Approval routing: site PM to regional to director of operations (or equivalent
  title in the org). No third-party layer.
- Reporting: site to regional to owner in a single chain. No TPM scorecard; the
  owner-oversight surfaces are implicit because the owner is also the operator.
- Staffing: regional-manager coverage is the owner's internal role; corporate
  support (marketing, learning, IT, compliance) is internal.
- Contract posture: vendor and service agreements are signed by the owner's
  authorized signatory per the approval matrix.

## What this overlay does not do

- Does not define approval thresholds; those live in the org overlay.
- Does not embed a staffing ratio; the ratio row lives in the reference layer.

## Interaction with other overlays

Loads alongside segment, form factor, and lifecycle overlays in the standard merge
order.
