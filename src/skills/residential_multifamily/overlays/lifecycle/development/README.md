# Development Lifecycle Overlay

Pre-construction posture. Applies from site control through construction-loan
closing (or equity-only ground-breaking) and the final pre-construction handoff to
the construction overlay.

## What this overlay shifts

- Reporting emphasis moves away from operating metrics toward entitlements,
  design, bid packages, and cost discipline vs. pro forma.
- Approval posture leans on development-manager, asset-manager, and executive
  review for material entitlement and design commitments.
- There is no site operating team; staffing is development-team plus consultants.

<a id="entitlement_calendar"></a>

## Entitlement calendar

- Track all jurisdictional milestones: zoning, site plan, architectural review,
  traffic, environmental, utility, stormwater, permits.
- Each milestone has an owner (consultant or in-house) and a drop-dead date tied
  to the critical path.
- Slippage outside the allowed float triggers escalation to asset management.

<a id="design_gates"></a>

## Design-coordination gates

- Schematic design, design development, and construction documents each have a
  sign-off gate. The gate reviews pro-forma alignment, value-engineering
  opportunities, operations input (especially service-standard and staffing
  constraints that design can affect), and maintainability of selected systems.
- Decisions that affect operability (e.g., finish package, unit layout, mechanical
  system, trash handling, package logistics) route to operations review before
  sign-off.

<a id="bid_package_readiness"></a>

## Bid-package readiness

- Bid packages are reviewed for completeness, clarity, and cost-risk allocation
  before going to market.
- Preferred-bidder lists are verified for license and insurance status.
- Bid-leveling framework is prepared before responses return.

## Transition to construction

When a GC is awarded and notice to proceed is issued, the construction overlay
loads alongside development. The construction overlay takes over the day-to-day
execution surface; development overlay remains active for residual entitlement or
design-change items.
