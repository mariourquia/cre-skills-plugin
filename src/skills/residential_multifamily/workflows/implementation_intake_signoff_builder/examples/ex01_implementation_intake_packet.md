# Implementation Intake Packet — Beacon Residential Southeast Rollout

## Purpose

Confirm the real source systems, export posture, field-level evidence, crosswalk ownership,
 reporting cadence, and approval gates needed to operationalize AppFolio, Sage Intacct,
 and Procore for Beacon Residential's Southeast portfolio.

## Scope Summary

- Portfolio scope: 14 multifamily properties in the Southeast.
- Systems in scope: AppFolio, Sage Intacct, Procore, and several manual finance packs.
- Primary consumers: implementation team, finance reporting, regional operations, COO, CFO.
- Decision gate: approve build only once source inventory, access model, field evidence,
  crosswalk owners, and reporting deadlines are explicit.

## Systems In Scope

- AppFolio Property Manager: property operations, rent roll, leasing, maintenance.
- Sage Intacct: GL, budget, forecast, entity reporting.
- Procore: project, draw, change order, and vendor data for active renovations.
- Manual owner packs: monthly owner reporting calendar and benchmark attachments.

## Operating Model Summary

- Southeast portfolio is self-managed operationally, with finance centralized.
- AppFolio is the system of record for property operations.
- Intacct is the system of record for corporate and property financials.
- Procore is active only for the renovation subset and must be cross-walked to Intacct
  project dimensions.

## Reporting Cadence Summary

- Month-end close begins on business day 1.
- Asset management review due business day 6.
- Owner reporting due business day 8.
- Investor packet due business day 10.
- Construction draw review every other Wednesday during active projects.

## Main Integration Assumptions

- Intacct project dimensions will remain the financial anchor for active renovations.
- AppFolio monthly delinquency packs remain manual until scheduled export access is approved.
- Existing property and entity mapping workbook remains the interim crosswalk source.

## Confidence and Blockers

- Confirmed by file or screenshot: source inventory, AppFolio export posture, Intacct budget access.
- Confirmed verbally only: final owner reporting cadence and exception approval path.
- Missing: current approved mapping workbook for vendors and projects.
- Blocked: Intacct project endpoint scope approval.

## Recommended Next Step

Approve a targeted follow-up to collect the vendor/project crosswalk workbook and final
 owner reporting calendar, then re-run the final sign-off preview.
