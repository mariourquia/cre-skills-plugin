The Sunrise sponsor needs a concise sign-off memo and a build-ready implementation intake packet. The portfolio is owner-oversight. AppFolio runs operations, Intacct runs finance, Procore runs construction, Dealpath runs the pipeline, GraySail may own benchmark refresh, Yardi still supports a legacy subset, and one region is still file-only through a third-party manager.

The sponsor wants:

- systems and environments covered
- what has been confirmed
- what is still assumed
- what is blocked
- top risks
- decisions requested
- approvals requested
- owners and dates
- recommendation

The build team also needs actual export evidence, crosswalk ownership, reporting cadence, and access model metadata.
