# Capital Call Notice Template + LP Onboarding Workflow

---

## TEMPLATE INSTRUCTIONS (strip before use)

This template produces a capital call notice and documents the full LP onboarding workflow from commitment to funding. Instructions appear in [BRACKETS]. Remove before distributing.

**Legal note**: Capital call notices are contractual documents governed by the LPA. Every field must match the LPA terms exactly. Have fund counsel review the first call notice before distribution.

**Timing**: Most LPAs require 10-15 business days advance notice for capital calls. Confirm your LPA's specific requirement.

## Stage 1: Pre-Commitment (Fundraise to Subscription)

### 1.1 Initial Outreach and Qualification

| Step | Action | Responsible | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|--------|------------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Send teaser / one-pager | Investor Relations | Day 0 | One-pager PDF |
| 2 | Confirm accredited / qualified purchaser status | Investor Relations | Day 1-3 | Verbal or written confirmation |
| 3 | Execute NDA | Fund Counsel | Day 3-7 | Signed NDA |
| 4 | Grant data room access (NDA tier) | Investor Relations | Day 7 | Data room credentials |
| 5 | Schedule initial meeting | Investor Relations | Day 7-14 | Calendar invite |
| 6 | Deliver pitch deck | GP | At meeting | Presentation |
| 7 | Follow-up: address questions, send FAQs | GP / IR | Day 14-21 | Email + FAQ document |

### 1.2 Diligence Phase

| Step | Action | Responsible | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|--------|------------|----------|-------------|
| 8 | Grant full data room access | Investor Relations | Upon request | Full data room credentials |
| 9 | Arrange reference calls (existing LPs) | GP / IR | Week 3-6 | Reference call schedule |
| 10 | Arrange operational DD call (asset mgmt, PM) | GP | Week 3-6 | Call with asset mgmt team |
| 11 | Respond to LP DDQ (due diligence questionnaire) | GP / Fund Counsel | Week 2-8 | Completed DDQ |
| 12 | Provide background check results | GP | Week 3-6 | Background check reports |
| 13 | LP counsel reviews LPA, negotiates side letter | Fund Counsel | Week 4-10 | Redline/clean LPA + side letter |
| 14 | Finalize side letter terms | GP + Fund Counsel | Week 6-12 | Executed side letter |

### 1.3 Subscription Execution

| Step | Action | Responsible | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|--------|------------|----------|-------------|
| 15 | LP completes subscription agreement | LP / LP Counsel | Week 8-14 | Signed subscription agreement |
| 16 | LP completes investor questionnaire (AML/KYC) | LP | Week 8-14 | Completed questionnaire |
| 17 | LP provides required documentation | LP | Week 8-14 | See documentation checklist below |
| 18 | GP reviews and accepts subscription | GP / Fund Counsel | Within 5 business days | Countersigned subscription |
| 19 | Confirm LP on investor register | Fund Administrator | Within 3 business days | Updated register |
| 20 | Send welcome letter with key contacts and dates | Investor Relations | Within 3 business days | Welcome letter |

## Stage 3: Post-Commitment -- Capital Call Cycle

### 3.1 Standard Capital Call Process

| Step | Action | Responsible | Timeline | Notes |
|------|--------|------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | GP identifies funding need | GP / Acquisitions | T-30 days | Upcoming acquisition, fees, or expenses |
| 2 | Prepare capital call schedule | Fund Accountant | T-20 days | Calculate per-LP amounts |
| 3 | Draft capital call notice | Fund Counsel + Accountant | T-18 days | Per LPA format |
| 4 | GP review and approval | GP Principal | T-15 days | Sign-off |
| 5 | Distribute notice to all LPs | Investor Relations | T-15 days (or per LPA) | Email + data room upload |
| 6 | LPs wire funds | LPs | T-0 (funding date) | Per wire instructions |
| 7 | Confirm receipt of all wires | Fund Accountant | T+1 | Bank verification |
| 8 | Follow up on any missing wires | Investor Relations | T+2 | Phone call to LP |
| 9 | Issue default notice if unfunded | Fund Counsel | T+[X] per LPA | Per default provisions |
| 10 | Update capital account statements | Fund Administrator | T+5 | Cumulative called, unfunded balance |

## Stage 4: Ongoing LP Servicing

### 4.1 Reporting Calendar

| Deliverable | Frequency | Due Date | Responsible |
|-------------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Quarterly investor update | Quarterly | 30-45 days after quarter-end | GP / IR |
| Capital account statement | Quarterly | With quarterly update | Fund Administrator |
| Annual audited financial statements | Annual | 90-120 days after fiscal year-end | Fund Auditor |
| Schedule K-1 | Annual | By March 15 (or extension deadline) | Fund Tax Accountant |
| Annual meeting / investor day | Annual | Q1 of following year | GP / IR |
| Ad hoc updates (acquisitions, dispositions) | As needed | Within 5 business days of event | GP / IR |

### 4.2 LP Communication Standards

| Scenario | Communication Method | Timing |
|----------|---------------------|--------|
| New acquisition closed | Email + IC memo in data room | Within 5 business days |
| Disposition closed | Email with deal summary and return attribution | Within 5 business days |
| Material adverse event (tenant default, natural disaster) | Phone call to advisory committee + email to all LPs | Within 48 hours |
| Capital call | Formal notice per LPA | Per LPA notice period |
| Distribution | Notice with amount and characterization (income vs. return of capital) | 5 business days before wire |
| GP personnel change | Email to all LPs | Within 10 business days |
| Quarterly update | Email with PDF attached + data room upload | Per reporting calendar |

## Stage 5: LP Offboarding (Transfer or Redemption)

### 5.1 Interest Transfer Process

[Most CRE funds are closed-end with limited transfer rights. The LPA governs.]

| Step | Action | Responsible | Notes |
|------|--------|------------|-------|
| 1 | LP notifies GP of intent to transfer | LP | Written notice per LPA |
| 2 | GP reviews transfer restrictions in LPA | Fund Counsel | ROFR, GP consent, minimum transfer size |
| 3 | GP offers ROFR to existing LPs (if required) | Investor Relations | Typically 30 days |
| 4 | If no ROFR exercise, GP reviews transferee | Fund Counsel | AML/KYC, accreditation, ERISA |
| 5 | GP consents or declines transfer | GP | Per LPA discretion |
| 6 | Execute assignment and assumption agreement | Fund Counsel | Transferor, transferee, GP sign |
| 7 | Update investor register | Fund Administrator | Reflect new LP |
| 8 | Issue capital account statement to both parties | Fund Administrator | Showing transfer date and values |
