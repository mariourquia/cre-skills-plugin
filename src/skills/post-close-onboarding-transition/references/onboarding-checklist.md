# 60-Day Post-Close Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose

Comprehensive day-by-day onboarding checklist for newly acquired CRE assets. Organized by phase with responsible party assignments and dependency tracking. Designed for institutional owner-operators acquiring stabilized, value-add, or lease-up properties.

## Day 60 Deliverables

At the end of the 60-day onboarding period, the following deliverables should be complete and presented to ownership:

### 1. Onboarding Completion Report

- Checklist completion percentage by category
- Outstanding items with resolution timeline
- Key findings vs. underwriting assumptions
- Risks identified and mitigation plans

### 2. Verified Rent Roll

- Lease-file-audited rent roll with all discrepancies resolved
- Critical dates calendar loaded and automated
- Estoppel status (received, pending, refused)
- Insurance compliance status by tenant

### 3. First Stabilized Budget

- Operating budget for remainder of fiscal year
- 5-year capital plan (Year 1 detailed, Years 2-5 projected)
- Variance analysis: actuals vs. acquisition underwriting
- Reserve adequacy assessment

### 4. Vendor Contract Summary

- Retained, renegotiated, and terminated contracts
- New vendor RFP status
- Projected annual vendor cost vs. underwriting assumption
- Insurance compliance status by vendor

### 5. 90-Day Action Plan

- Priority capital projects with timeline and budget
- Leasing strategy and marketing plan
- Tenant engagement plan
- Outstanding issues requiring ownership decision
