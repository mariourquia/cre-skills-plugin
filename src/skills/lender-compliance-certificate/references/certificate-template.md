### Section 1: Certification Statement

The undersigned, [NAME], [TITLE] of [BORROWER ENTITY NAME] ("Borrower"), hereby certifies to [LENDER NAME] ("Lender") as follows, pursuant to Section [SECTION] of the Loan Agreement:

1. I am the [TITLE] of Borrower and am authorized to execute and deliver this Compliance Certificate on behalf of Borrower.

2. I have reviewed the terms of the Loan Agreement and have made, or caused to be made under my supervision, a review of the activities and financial condition of Borrower and the Property during the Reporting Period.

3. Based on such review, as of the date hereof and during the Reporting Period:

   (a) No Event of Default, or event which with the giving of notice or lapse of time or both would constitute an Event of Default, has occurred and is continuing [OR: except as described in Exhibit A attached hereto];

   (b) Borrower has performed and observed all covenants, agreements, and conditions required to be performed or observed under the Loan Agreement;

   (c) The representations and warranties contained in the Loan Agreement remain true and correct in all material respects;

   (d) The financial information and calculations set forth herein are true, correct, and complete in all material respects.

### Worked Example -- All Calculations

**Property**: Oakwood Office Center, 150,000 SF, Dallas, TX
**Loan**: $22,000,000, 4.75% fixed, 30-yr amortizing, 10-yr term
**Reporting Period**: Q4 2025 (Oct-Dec), with T-12 through Dec 2025

**DSCR Calculation:**

| Line | Item | Amount |
|------|------|--------|
| A | Gross Potential Revenue (T-12) | $4,125,000 |
| B | Vacancy and Credit Loss | ($206,250) |
| C | Effective Gross Revenue | $3,918,750 |
| D | Operating Expenses (T-12) | ($1,567,500) |
| E | **NOI** | **$2,351,250** |
| F | Less: Capital Reserve ($0.25/SF) | ($37,500) |
| G | Less: TI/LC Reserve ($1.00/SF) | ($150,000) |
| H | **NCF** | **$2,163,750** |
| I | Annual Debt Service (P&I) | $1,376,832 |
| J | **DSCR (NCF basis)** | **1.57x** |

Required: >= 1.25x. Status: PASS.

**LTV Calculation:**

| Line | Item | Amount |
|------|------|--------|
| A | Outstanding Principal | $21,340,000 |
| B | Appraised Value (Jun 2025) | $32,500,000 |
| C | **LTV** | **65.7%** |

Required: <= 75%. Status: PASS.

**Debt Yield Calculation:**

| Line | Item | Amount |
|------|------|--------|
| A | NOI (T-12) | $2,351,250 |
| B | Outstanding Principal | $21,340,000 |
| C | **Debt Yield** | **11.02%** |

Required: >= 8.5%. Status: PASS.

**Occupancy:**

| Line | Item | Amount |
|------|------|--------|
| A | Total NRA | 150,000 SF |
| B | Occupied SF | 138,750 SF |
| C | **Physical Occupancy** | **92.5%** |

Required: >= 85%. Status: PASS.
