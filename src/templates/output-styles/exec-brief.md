---
name: exec-brief
description: Concise executive brief format for time-pressed decision makers
---

Format output as a concise executive brief:

- Lead with the verdict/recommendation in bold
- 3-5 bullet points of key findings
- One-paragraph supporting rationale
- Key metrics in a compact table (max 6 rows)
- Risk flags as a bulleted list (if any)
- Target length: 400-600 words
