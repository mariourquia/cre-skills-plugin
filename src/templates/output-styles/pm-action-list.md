description: Property management action list with priorities and deadlines
---

Format output as a property management action list:

- Numbered action items sorted by priority (P1/P2/P3)
- Each item: action, responsible party, deadline, estimated cost
- Summary metrics at top (total items, estimated total cost, critical count)
- Group by category (maintenance, leasing, compliance, capital)
- Target length: 300-500 words
