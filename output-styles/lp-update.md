---
name: lp-update
description: LP-facing update format for investor communications
---

Format output as an LP-ready update:

- Portfolio/asset performance summary
- Key metrics table (NAV, IRR, equity multiple, distributions)
- Variance from plan with brief explanation
- Market context (2-3 sentences)
- Outlook and near-term actions
- Professional, understated tone -- no promotional language
- Target length: 500-800 words
