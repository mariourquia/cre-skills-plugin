---

Format output as an investment committee memo:

- Executive summary (1 paragraph, verdict first)
- Investment thesis (2-3 paragraphs)
- Market and submarket context
- Financial analysis with full metrics table
- Risk factors with probability and impact assessment
- Sensitivity analysis summary
- Recommendation with conditions
- Target length: 1500-2500 words
