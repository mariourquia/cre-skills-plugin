# Disposition Checklist: Pre-Sale Preparation by Workstream

---

## Purpose

This checklist covers every material task required to prepare a CRE asset for sale, organized by workstream and sequenced by timeline. The goal is to maximize sale price by eliminating buyer objections, compressing diligence timelines, and presenting a clean, auditable asset that commands a premium in the market.

**When to start**: 6-9 months before target listing date. Rushing pre-sale prep compresses the timeline and forces concessions.

**Who owns this**: Asset Manager, with support from Property Manager, Legal Counsel, and the Accounting/Finance team.

---

## Timeline Overview

| Phase | Timing | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Phase 1: Internal Assessment | T-9 to T-6 months | Evaluate asset condition, financial performance, market timing |
| Phase 2: Property Preparation | T-6 to T-3 months | Physical condition, deferred maintenance, curb appeal |
| Phase 3: Financial Package | T-5 to T-3 months | Clean financials, rent roll, T-12 reconciliation |
| Phase 4: Legal and Diligence | T-4 to T-2 months | Title, surveys, environmental, lease abstracts |
| Phase 5: Marketing Launch | T-3 to T-2 months | Broker selection, OM preparation, buyer list |
| Phase 6: Sale Execution | T-2 to Close | Offers, negotiation, PSA, buyer diligence, closing |

## Critical Path Items

These items are on the critical path. Delay on any of them will delay listing or closing.

| Item | Why Critical | Typical Lead Time |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Phase I ESA | Must be <12 months old; takes 3-4 weeks to complete | 4-6 weeks |
| ALTA Survey | Takes 3-6 weeks; buyers will not close without it | 6-8 weeks |
| Title report clearance | Unresolved exceptions can take months to cure | 4-12 weeks |
| Estoppel certificates | Tenant cooperation is unpredictable; 30-60 days typical | 4-8 weeks |
| Property condition repairs | Deferred maintenance discovered late forces price cuts | Variable |
| Loan payoff / assumption | Lender response time is 30-60 days | 4-8 weeks |
| OM preparation | Broker needs 3-4 weeks with clean data | 3-4 weeks |
