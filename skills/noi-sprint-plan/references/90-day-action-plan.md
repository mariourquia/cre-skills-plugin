# 90-Day NOI Sprint Action Plan

---

## Purpose

This template creates a week-by-week operating plan for the first 90 days of an NOI improvement initiative. It converts the NOI bridge into specific tasks with deadlines, owners, and KPIs. The 90-day sprint is the execution layer -- the bridge tells you what to do; the sprint plan tells you when, who, and how to track progress.

**When to use**: Immediately post-acquisition (Day 1 of ownership), or when launching a portfolio-wide NOI improvement program.

**Cadence**: Weekly check-ins (30 minutes) with the asset management and property management teams. Monthly reporting to IC/investors.

---

## Part 1: Sprint Structure

### Week-by-Week Framework

| Week | Phase | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1-2 | Assessment | Walk the property. Audit every lease, vendor contract, and expense line |
| 3-4 | Quick Wins | Implement changes requiring zero capex and minimal approval |
| 5-8 | Medium Initiatives | Launch renovation, RUBS, insurance rebid, vendor renegotiation |
| 9-12 | Sustain and Scale | Measure results, adjust, and set up quarterly reporting cadence |
| 13 | Report | 90-day report to IC with actuals vs. plan and forward outlook |

**Goal**: Understand the actual condition of every revenue and expense line. Do not rely on seller financials alone.

## Part 4: Escalation Triggers

Define conditions that trigger escalation to GP/IC:

| Trigger | Threshold | Escalation Action |
|---------|----------|------------------|
| Occupancy drops below | [X]% (e.g., 88%) | Weekly AM/PM call; review pricing strategy |
| NOI falls more than [X]% below plan | 10% below monthly plan | IC update memo within 5 business days |
| Renovation cost overrun exceeds | 15% of unit budget | GC meeting; rebid remaining units |
| Renovation timeline slips by | 4+ weeks | Revised schedule to IC; consider adding crew |
| Delinquency exceeds | 8% of GPR | Accelerate collection/eviction process |
| Insurance claim or casualty event | Any material event | Same-day notification to GP; activate insurance |
| Local regulatory change | Rent control, moratorium | Legal analysis within 48 hours |
| Key staff departure | PM, maintenance lead | Replacement plan within 1 week |
